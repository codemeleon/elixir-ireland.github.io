VIBE Collaborations

The Virtual Infrastructure for BioElixir (VIBE) enables collaborative projects between ELIXIR Ireland members and external partners. Join innovative bioinformatics projects even if you're not yet a formal ELIXIR Ireland member.

What is VIBE?

VIBE is a flexible collaboration framework that allows researchers, institutions, and organizations to participate in specific ELIXIR Ireland projects and initiatives. It provides a pathway for engagement without requiring full membership, making it ideal for:

  • Researchers exploring collaborative opportunities
  • International partners on specific projects
  • Industry partners seeking bioinformatics expertise
  • Organizations testing ELIXIR Ireland services
  • Groups transitioning toward full membership
