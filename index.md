# Welcome to ELIXIR Ireland

ELIXIR Ireland is a national Node of the European ELIXIR infrastructure.Our mission is to coordinate and develop bioinformatics resources to support life science research and its translation to medicine, agriculture, bioindustries, and society.

## Our Services
- Data management
- Training and education
- Tools and software
