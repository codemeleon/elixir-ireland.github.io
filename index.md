# Welcome to ELIXIR Ireland

ELIXIR Ireland is a national node of the European ELIXIR infrastructure, which supports life science research and its translation to medicine, agriculture, bioindustries, and society. Our mission is to coordinate and develop bioinformatics resources to support life science research and its translation to medicine, agriculture, bioindustries, and society.

## Our Services

- Data management
- Training and education
- Tools and software
- Compute resources
