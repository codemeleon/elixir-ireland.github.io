About ELIXIR Ireland

ELIXIR Europe

ELIXIR is an intergovernmental organisation that brings together life science resources from across Europe. These resources include databases, software tools, training materials, cloud storage and supercomputers. The goal of ELIXIR is to coordinate, integrate and sustain bioinformatics resources across its member states and enable users in academia and industry to access services that are vital for their research.

ELIXIR Ireland

ELIXIR Ireland is the national Node of the pan-European ELIXIR Research Infrastructure, serving as Ireland's gateway to world-class bioinformatics resources and expertise. We coordinate computational biology activities across Irish research institutions, providing essential data management, analysis tools, and training to advance life science research throughout the country.
