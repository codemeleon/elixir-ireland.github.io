What we do

ELIXIR Ireland provides essential infrastructure, services, and support for life science research across Ireland and beyond.

Services

ELIXIR Ireland maintains and develops key computational services and tools that support life science research. Our services are designed to be FAIR (Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, and Reusable) and are integrated with the broader ELIXIR infrastructure.

Data Resources

We provide access to curated biological databases and data repositories that support research across multiple domains.

Computational Tools

Our portfolio includes bioinformatics tools and pipelines for data analysis, visualization, and interpretation.

Cloud Infrastructure

We offer scalable computational resources and storage solutions for data-intensive research projects.

Support Services

Expert consultation and technical support to help researchers leverage computational approaches effectively.

Projects

ELIXIR Ireland participates in national and international collaborative projects that advance life science research infrastructure and capabilities. Our projects span data management, interoperability, training, and tool development.

We work closely with research institutions, industry partners, and international collaborators to deliver innovative solutions that address key challenges in computational biology and bioinformatics.

Through our project work, we contribute to building a sustainable ecosystem for life science data and ensure Irish researchers have access to world-class resources and expertise.

Training

ELIXIR Ireland is committed to building bioinformatics capacity through comprehensive training programmes. We offer workshops, courses, and online resources covering fundamental to advanced topics in computational biology.

Training Activities

  • Hands-on workshops and courses
  • Online tutorials and learning materials
  • Train-the-trainer programmes
  • Webinars and seminars
  • Summer schools and intensive courses

Topics Covered

  • Data analysis and statistics
  • Genomics and transcriptomics
  • Proteomics and metabolomics
  • Data management and FAIR principles
  • Programming and computational skills

Travel Grants

ELIXIR Ireland supports the mobility of researchers and bioinformaticians through travel grants. These grants enable Irish researchers to attend international conferences, training events, and collaborative meetings within the ELIXIR network.

By facilitating participation in international events, we help build networks, foster collaborations, and ensure Irish researchers remain at the forefront of developments in computational life sciences.

Information about available travel grants, eligibility criteria, and application procedures is regularly shared with our community.

Knowledge Exchange

Knowledge exchange is central to ELIXIR Ireland's mission. We facilitate the sharing of expertise, best practices, and resources between academia, industry, and healthcare sectors.

Through seminars, workshops, networking events, and collaborative projects, we create opportunities for researchers, clinicians, and industry professionals to connect, share insights, and develop innovative solutions to life science challenges.

Our knowledge exchange activities help translate research findings into practical applications and ensure that computational approaches are accessible to all stakeholders in the life science community.

International Working Group Involvement

ELIXIR Ireland actively participates in international working groups and collaborative initiatives that shape the future of life science research infrastructure and data standards.

RDA

Research Data Alliance - contributing to the development of data sharing practices and infrastructure for research.

GA4GH

Global Alliance for Genomics and Health - advancing responsible genomic data sharing for human health.

OHDSI

Observational Health Data Sciences and Informatics - improving health through large-scale analysis of health data.

MLCommons

Engineering the future of machine learning through benchmarking and best practices for AI systems.

Through these partnerships, we ensure that Irish researchers benefit from global standards, best practices, and collaborative networks while contributing Irish expertise to international initiatives.

Research Data & Software Management

ELIXIR Ireland provides guidance and support for effective research data and software management, ensuring that research outputs are findable, accessible, interoperable, and reusable (FAIR).

Data Management

  • Data Management Plan (DMP) support
  • FAIR data principles implementation
  • Data storage and preservation solutions
  • Metadata standards and ontologies
  • Data sharing and publication guidance

Software Management

  • Version control and reproducibility
  • Software documentation best practices
  • Containerization and workflow management
  • Software licensing and citation
  • Code review and quality assurance

We work with researchers to develop strategies that ensure their data and software are managed effectively throughout the research lifecycle, maximizing the impact and longevity of research outputs.
