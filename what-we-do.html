What we do

ELIXIR Ireland provides essential infrastructure, services, and support for life science research across Ireland and beyond.

Services

ELIXIR Ireland maintains and develops key computational services and tools that support life science research. Our services are designed to be FAIR (Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, and Reusable) and are integrated with the broader ELIXIR infrastructure.

Data Resources

We provide access to curated biological databases and data repositories that support research across multiple domains.

Computational Tools

Our portfolio includes bioinformatics tools and pipelines for data analysis, visualization, and interpretation.

Support Services

Expert consultation and technical support to help researchers leverage computational approaches effectively.

Projects

ELIXIR Ireland participates in national and international collaborative projects that advance life science research infrastructure and capabilities. Our projects span data management, interoperability, training, and tool development.

GDI Ireland

The Irish node of the Genomic Data Infrastructure (GDI) project, enabling access to genomic and related health data across Europe.

Genome of Europe Ireland

Part of a large-scale, cross-border project to generate a European network of national genomic reference cohorts.

ELIXIR STEERS

Supporting training and capacity building in bioinformatics across the ELIXIR network through staff and knowledge exchange.

SFI Centre - Genomics Data Science

Ireland's national centre for genomics data science research, developing computational methods and infrastructure for genomic medicine.

Training

ELIXIR Ireland is committed to building bioinformatics capacity through comprehensive training programmes. We offer workshops, courses, and online resources covering fundamental to advanced topics in computational biology. Find our upcoming events or browse on-demand training resources.

Research Data & Software Management

ELIXIR Ireland provides guidance for effective research data and software management, ensuring outputs are findable, accessible, interoperable, and reusable (FAIR).

Research Data Management

We guide researchers in making their data FAIR. For comprehensive resources, explore the ELIXIR Research Data Management Kit (RDMkit).

  • Data Management Plan (DMP) support
  • FAIR data principles implementation
  • Guidance on data storage, preservation, and sharing

Research Software Management

We help researchers improve the quality and sustainability of their software. Discover best practices in the Research Software Quality Kit (RSQkit).

  • Version control and reproducibility
  • Software documentation and licensing
  • Containerization and workflow management

International Working Group Involvement

ELIXIR Ireland actively participates in international working groups and collaborative initiatives that shape the future of life science research infrastructure and data standards.

RDA

Research Data Alliance - contributing to the development of data sharing practices and infrastructure for research.

GA4GH

Global Alliance for Genomics and Health - advancing responsible genomic data sharing for human health.

OHDSI

Observational Health Data Sciences and Informatics - improving health through large-scale analysis of health data.

MLCommons

Engineering the future of machine learning through benchmarking and best practices for AI systems.

Travel Grants & Knowledge Exchange Opportunities

ELIXIR Ireland supports engagements such as event attendance and brokers opportunities for knowledge exchange to connect researchers, industry partners, and international collaborators. Contact us to explore how we can support your project and engagement needs.
