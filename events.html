Events

    Annual Virtual Institute for Bioinformatics and Evolution (VIBE) meeting brings together experts in bioinformatics and evolutionary biology for talks, networking, and collaboration. The Virtual Institute of Bioinformatics & Evolution (VIBE) was founded in 2000, to consolidate bioinformatics research efforts in Ireland, provide a forum for emerging bioinformatics researchers, provide a platform for the development of an Irish commercial bioinformatics capability, and develop bioinformatics teaching within Irish Universities. Join the VIBE mailing list to be informed of announcement and upcoming meetings.
