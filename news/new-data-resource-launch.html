February 28, 2024

New Data Resource Launched for Irish Researchers

ELIXIR Ireland is proud to announce the launch of the Irish Biological Data Repository (IBDR), a comprehensive new platform designed to provide Irish researchers with seamless access to genomic datasets, bioinformatics tools, and computational resources. This initiative marks a significant milestone in strengthening Ireland's bioinformatics infrastructure.

About the Repository

The IBDR is a centralized platform that aggregates biological data from multiple sources, making it easier for researchers to discover, access, and analyze genomic and molecular data. The repository is built on FAIR (Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, and Reusable) data principles, ensuring that all datasets meet international standards for data management and sharing.

Key Features

The new data resource offers several innovative features designed to enhance research productivity:

  • Unified search interface across multiple data types and sources
  • Integration with popular bioinformatics analysis tools
  • Secure data storage with controlled access for sensitive datasets
  • API access for programmatic data retrieval
  • Data visualization tools for exploratory analysis
  • Metadata standards compliance and validation

Supporting Irish Research

This initiative directly supports the Irish research community by reducing barriers to data access and promoting collaboration between institutions. Researchers across Ireland can now easily share their data while maintaining appropriate access controls and attribution.

The repository currently hosts over 500 datasets spanning genomics, transcriptomics, proteomics, and metabolomics studies. These datasets represent research conducted at institutions across Ireland and include both publicly available and access-controlled data collections.

Getting Started

Irish researchers can register for access to the IBDR through their institutional credentials. Training materials, documentation, and video tutorials are available to help users navigate the platform and make the most of its features.

We will be hosting a series of online workshops throughout March and April to demonstrate the platform's capabilities and answer questions from the research community. Registration links will be available on our training page.

Future Development

The IBDR is continuously evolving based on user feedback and emerging needs. Future enhancements will include machine learning-powered data discovery, enhanced collaboration features, and integration with additional European data resources through the ELIXIR network.

For more information about the Irish Biological Data Repository, please visit our resources page or contact our support team at support@elixir-ireland.org.
