March 15, 2024

ELIXIR Ireland Bioinformatics Workshop 2024

We are excited to announce the ELIXIR Ireland Bioinformatics Workshop 2024, taking place from June 10-12 at University College Dublin. This flagship event brings together researchers, bioinformaticians, and data scientists from across Ireland and Europe to share knowledge, collaborate, and advance the field of computational biology.

Event Highlights

The three-day workshop will feature an impressive lineup of keynote speakers, interactive workshops, and poster sessions. Attendees will have the opportunity to learn about the latest developments in bioinformatics, genomics, and data management while networking with leading experts in the field.

Key sessions include:

  • Advanced RNA-seq analysis techniques
  • Machine learning applications in bioinformatics
  • FAIR data principles and implementation
  • Cloud computing for large-scale biological data
  • Single-cell genomics workflows

Who Should Attend

This workshop is designed for bioinformaticians, computational biologists, researchers working with biological data, PhD students, postdocs, and anyone interested in learning about modern approaches to biological data analysis. Both beginners and experienced practitioners will find valuable content tailored to their level.

Registration Information

Registration is now open and spaces are limited. Early bird registration is available until April 30th. The workshop fee includes access to all sessions, workshop materials, lunches, and the networking reception on June 11th.

For more information and to register, please visit our events page or contact us at info@elixir-ireland.org.

Venue and Accommodation

The workshop will be held at the Conway Institute of Biomolecular and Biomedical Research at University College Dublin. The venue offers state-of-the-art facilities and is easily accessible by public transport. A list of recommended nearby hotels will be provided to registered participants.
