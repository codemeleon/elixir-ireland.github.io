VIBE – The All Ireland Bioinformatics Network

The Virtual Institute of Bioinformatics and Evolution

Uniting the bioinformatics and computational biology community across the island of Ireland.

VIBE is a grassroots, bottom-up community network representing bioinformatics researchers, data scientists, and computational biologists across Ireland. Since its inception, VIBE has served as the primary forum for networking, collaboration, and the exchange of ideas in the Irish life sciences data sector.

Governance

We operate under a shared set of principles to ensure transparency and community-led decision-making. The VIBE Constitution outlines our purpose, membership structure, and operating procedures.

View the VIBE Constitution on Zenodo

Strategic Context: VIBE & ELIXIR

The All-Island Connection

VIBE operates as an All-Island network, bridging the gap between researchers in the Republic of Ireland and Northern Ireland. This unique position allows us to foster deep collaboration between:

  • ELIXIR Ireland: Supporting the infrastructure, data stewardship, and training needs of the community.
  • ELIXIR UK: Connecting our Northern Ireland partners to the broader UK bioinformatics landscape.

Why this matters

While ELIXIR provides the European-scale infrastructure and funding mechanisms, VIBE provides the community. We are the people, the labs, and the students driving the research. VIBE acts as the community voice that informs ELIXIR Ireland's strategic direction.
